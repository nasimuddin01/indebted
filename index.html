Who We Are

We are a group of technologists, engineers, data scientists, marketers and client service specialists who obsess about one thing; improving client outcomes when it comes to debt collection and receivables management.

Our Story

We were founded on the concept that traditional debt collection is broken. It has failed to adapt in how customers want to communicate, poorly leverages data to understand preferences for servicing overdue accounts, and most importantly, offers a bad customer experience.

We put the customer at the forefront of everything we do. We understand them, support them, and provide a transparent and simple process to resolve their accounts.
